Step-by-step farming advice

Each guide includes preparation, planting, maintenance, and harvest recommendations for smarter farm management.

1. Prepare the soil

  • Test soil for pH and nutrients.
  • Choose the right compost and organic amendments.
  • Use cover crops to build structure and fertility.

2. Choose the right seeds

  • Select varieties suited to local climate and water availability.
  • Balance early, mid, and late-season crops for steady harvests.
  • Use certified seeds when possible to improve yield reliability.

3. Maintain healthy growth

  • Monitor irrigation with a schedule based on crop needs.
  • Scout for pests and disease weekly.
  • Feed crops with balanced nutrients and natural soil boosters.

4. Harvest with confidence

  • Harvest at the peak of maturity for best quality.
  • Handle produce gently to reduce damage and losses.
  • Store and transport with clean, dry conditions to preserve value.
